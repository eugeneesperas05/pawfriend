About Us

Welcome to PawFriend! We are dedicated to helping pets in need find their forever homes. Browse our selection of dogs and cats available for adoption and take the first step in giving them the love and care they deserve. click here

Our Mission

To provide a safe and loving environment for dogs and cats in need, and to match them with caring and responsible owners who will provide them with a forever home.

Our Vision

To create a world where every dog and cat or any animals has a loving home and wonderful life, and where animal welfare is a top priority for individuals and society.

Our Values

  • Compassion
  • Responsibility
  • Dedication
  • Collaboration
  • Transparency

Our Team

Behind PawFriend is a team of dedicated animal lovers who are passionate about providing a safe and loving environment for our furry friends. From our adoption counselors to our volunteers, each member of our team is committed to helping dogs and cats in need find their forever homes. We work tirelessly to ensure the best possible care for our animals and to create a positive adoption experience for our clients. Meet our team and join us in our mission to make a difference in the lives of pets and their owners.

Our History

PawFriend Adoption Pet was founded in March 2023 by WD55 class from KodeGo especially Mr. E. Esperas an animal lover who were concerned about the high number of dogs and cats in shelters who were in need of forever homes. With a mission to improve animal welfare and promote responsible pet ownership, the founders of PawFriend Adoption Pet worked tirelessly to establish the organization and develop programs and services to support its mission.

How PawFriend Started?

there was a student who was passionate about animals. He often spent his free time volunteering at local animal shelters, playing with cats and dogs, and learning about their different personalities and needs. One day, his teacher assigned them a mini-project: to create a website that promotes their interest and hobbies. This student is excited about the idea and immediately started brainstorming. He wanted his website to be different from others, and he wanted it to truly make a difference in the lives of animals. He spent hours researching and gathering information, and after weeks of hard work, he finally launched his website: Pawfriend. The website featured heartwarming stories of cats and dogs who were once homeless but found their forever homes through adoption. It also provided helpful tips for potential pet owners, such as how to choose the right pet for their lifestyle and how to take care of their new furry friend. At first, the website received only a few visitors since the website is using a personal link, because it's not up to the internet at the moment, but the student didn't give up. He continued to spread the word about Pawfriend, sharing it on social media, and even reaching out to his local barangay about his project. Slowly but surely, more people started visiting the website and sharing its message. The student were ecstatic when he received his first success story: a family who had adopted a dog from PawFriend shelter after reading about Pawfriend. The family had been hesitant about adopting a pet, but after reading about the joy and love that a furry companion could bring, they decided to take the leap. From that moment on, Pawfriend will continue to grow and inspire others to adopt pets in need. Adoption Success story

"I have fond memories of my childhood, when my father compassionately cared for numerous stray cats and dogs, even nursing those in dire condition back to health. This nurturing behavior left a lasting impression on me. Subsequently, these feral animals began awaiting our presence every morning. It's clear that my love for animals is an inheritance from my father. Skipping ahead to 2011, I encountered Puma, a distressed cat, in a sorry state on the streets. Without a moment's hesitation, I brought her into our home, provided her with necessary medical attention, and thankfully, she made a full recovery. Puma's eyes are truly distinctive – one is a striking blue, while the other boasts a warm yellowish-brown hue. In 2016, when Puma turned five, she gave birth to three endearing kittens. Remarkably, one of them inherited her captivating heterochromatic eyes, quickly becoming the favorite within our family. This kitten, whom I affectionately named Oreo, has remained by my side ever since. Oreo has now reached the age of six and is currently expecting her own litter. While some may consider cats with such unique eyes to be valuable, I could never put a price on Oreo. The immense happiness and joy she has brought to my family make her an invaluable part of our lives – my eternal fur baby."
