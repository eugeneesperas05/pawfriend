MAKE A DONATION

PawFriend Foundation is a non-profit organization that is dedicated to rescuing and providing care for abandoned, abused, and neglected animals. As an animal lover, you may want to support our cause by making a donation to help us continue our vital work. Making a donation to PawFriend Foundation is a simple and meaningful way to show your support. You can make a donation online through our website www.pawfriend.com, or by mailing a check or money order to PawFriend shelter address (#7008 Concepcion, Tarlac). When making a donation, you will have the option to specify the amount you would like to give, as well as whether you would like to make a one-time or recurring donation. Your donation will help PawFriend Foundation provide food, shelter, medical care, and other necessities for animals in need. Every dollar you give will go directly to our programs, as the organization is run entirely by volunteers and has no administrative costs. By making a donation to PawFriend Foundation, you will be making a difference in the lives of animals who have been abandoned or mistreated. Your contribution will help ensure that they receive the care and love they deserve, and that they have a second chance at a happy life. Thank you for considering a donation to PawFriend Foundation, and for your compassion and kindness towards animals.
