About Us

Welcome to PawFriend! We are dedicated to helping pets in need find their forever homes. Browse our selection of dogs and cats available for adoption and take the first step in giving them the love and care they deserve. click here

Our Mission

To provide a safe and loving environment for dogs and cats in need, and to match them with caring and responsible owners who will provide them with a forever home.

Our Vision

To create a world where every dog and cat or any animals has a loving home and wonderful life, and where animal welfare is a top priority for individuals and society.

Our Values

  • Compassion
  • Responsibility
  • Dedication
  • Collaboration
  • Transparency
